CHAIN OF EVENTS:

- email --> PDF attachment --> link from PDF --> Initial URL --> redirects to phishing site impersonating Amazon

NOTES:

- We recently collected 31 PDF files with links to phishing sites impersonating Amazon.
- During our investigation, none of the associated PDF documents we found had yet been submitted to VirusTotal.
- Initial URLs from the PDF files redirect to sub-domains of duckdns[.]org that hosts the phishing website.
- These phishing websites use cloaking to redirect scans and other analysis attempts to benign domains.
- Domains for most of the initial URLs and intermediate staging URLs are hosted on the same IP address.
