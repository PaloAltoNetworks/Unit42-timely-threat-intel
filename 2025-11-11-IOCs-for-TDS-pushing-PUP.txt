NOTES:

- We've been investigating traffic direction system (TDS) activity pushing potentially unwanted programs (PUP)
- This TDS activity features multi-layer anti-bot protection to evade detection, which includes:
  -- anti-bot captcha
  -- multiple fingerprinting libraries (ThumbmarkJS and FingerprintJS)

DETAILS:

- This campaign takes advantage of people searching for free versions of copyrighted material
  -- Includes cracked versions of popular software or video games
- Initial websites in this URL chain often use CAPTCHA pages to avoid web crawlers and other types of automated scans.
- These websites often have additional fingerprinting and bot detection using multiple fingerprinting libraries.
- Once the traffic passes fingerprinting and anti-bot requirements, it triggers a redirection chain to the final download.
- The redirection chain uses intermediary URLs that lead, and to the final download download pages.
  -- intermediary URLs hosted on mqdownload[.]com or yuishare[.]com
  -- Final download pages hosted on locked-link[.]com, installxcheck[.]com or ggclicker[.]com
- Upon clicking a download button, the page guides the visitor to finish an offer before downloading the file. 
- Downloads are usually specialized versions of web browsers or browser extensions.
