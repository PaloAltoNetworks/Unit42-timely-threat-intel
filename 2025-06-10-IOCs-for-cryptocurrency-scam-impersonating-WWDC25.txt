2025-06-10 (TUESDAY): CRYPTOCURRENCY SCAM SITE IMPERSONATING WWDC25

AUTHOR:

- Shu Wang

NOTES:

- A scam site wwdc25[.]com, which is registered on 2025-06-07, is impersonating the official WWDC25 event.
- This site is impersonating Apple's 2025 World Wide Developer's Conference.
- This site is promoting a fake cryptocurrency giveaway to steal funds from potential victims.
- The scam asks victims to transfer cryptocurrency to a wallet the scammers control
- The scam claims victims will receive double their cryptocurrency amount sent to any of the scammers wallets.
- The site currently impersonates or uses a CloudFlare platform challenge to avoid detection through web crawlers.
