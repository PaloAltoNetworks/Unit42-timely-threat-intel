2026-02-06 (FRIDAY): SCAMS AND PREDATORY SERVICES RELATED TO THE 2026 SUPER BOWL

AUTHORS:

- Reethika Ramesh, Daiping Liu

NOTES:

- Beware of scam sites and predatory services related to Super Bowl LX.
- These sites include several unofficial ticket websites.
- These also include predatory services claim to help with cancelling streaming services. 
- Stay vigilant and only use official and verified vendors!

DETAILS:

- Predatory services have appeared that claim to send official account cancellation notices to streaming services related to the Super Bowl including Peacock and YoutubeTV.
- They collect user information along with a payment of $21.95 to send cancellation notices to the companies.
- While these services are not necessarily malicious, monetized landing pages that link to these services are designed to take advantage of the buzz surrounding the Super Bowl event.
- Several unofficial ticket websites may lead to malicious pages or collect payment without actually giving you any tickets.
- Several websites advertising tickets may lead to unauthorized vendors that disclose they are unofficial sites.
- Users should attempt to procure tickets exclusively through authorized, verified vendors and official platforms for a safe experience.
