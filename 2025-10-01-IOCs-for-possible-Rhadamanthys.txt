AUTHORS:

NOTES:

- This is a long-running campaign that has distributed malware disguised as:
  -- Cracked versions of popular software
  -- Other copyright-protected material
- This campaign uses password-protected 7-Zip archives to avoid detection.
- The extracted EXE misuses AutoIt with CypherIT encryption to install possible Rhadamthys malware.
- This particular infection chain has previously distributed Lumma Stealer.
- We originally reported on this activity in May 2025, although it was active before that:
  -- https://github.com/PaloAltoNetworks/Unit42-timely-threat-intel/blob/main/2025-05-19-IOCs-for-CypherIT-and-AutoIt-used-in-distribution-of-Lumma-Stealer.txt
