2025-11-25 (TUESDAY): DOMAINS USED FOR BLACK FRIDAY SCAMS

AUTHORS:

- Reethika Ramesh, Daiping Liu

NOTES:

- Beware of scams related to Black Friday (the Friday after the US Thanksgiving holiday).
- On this day, retailers often offer sales and discounts for the upcoming holiday season.
- Known threat actors are increasing their phishing emails related to Black Friday.
- These emails often link to fraudulent luxury shopping sites. 
- We have also noticed a rise in the registration of new domains used for suspicious or malicious activities.

DETAILS:

- Known threat actors, like those associated with luxury shopping fraud, are intensifying their phishing efforts.
- We've noticed an increasing volume of spam and phishing emails tailored for Black Friday.
