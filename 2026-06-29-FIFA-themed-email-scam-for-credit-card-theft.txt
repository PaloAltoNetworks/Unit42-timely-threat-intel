2026-06-29 (MONDAY): FIFA-THEMED EMAIL SCAM FOR CREDIT CARD THEFT

AUTHOR:

- Amanda Tanner

NOTES:

- We have identified a phishing/scam campaign targeting personally identifiable information (PII)/payment card details.
- This campaign takes advantage of the hype around the 2026 FIFA World Cup by using "Champion Reward" survey lures.
- Of note, these email lures and associated web pages are not at all affiliated with FIFA.
- The emails come from an authentication-passing mailer at adfluxi[.]com. 
- Recipients click on links that redirect to decoy sites or fake "reward" pages.
  -- These URLs detect sandboxes and non-US visitors and route them to decoy sites.
  -- But the URLs send real US visitors to fake "reward" pages. 
- Those pages harvest users' PII like full payment card details (PAN/CVV/expiry).
- These pages exfiltrate the user data to a URL at hxxps[:]//gocellbel[.]com/api/orders. 
- The pages are monetized via the operator's affiliate ID 1189. 
- No malware or password phishing; the goal is PII and payment card theft.
