2025-09-09 (TUESDAY): SCAM DOMAINS RELATED TO 2026 FIFA WORLD CUP

AUTHORS:

- Reethika Ramesh, Daiping Liu, Zhanhao Chen

REFERENCES:

- https://www.linkedin.com/posts/unit42_fifaworldcup-activity-7372359639381757952-45J4/

NOTES:

- Threat actors are registering domains that seem related to the 2026 FIFA World Cup. 
- Sites for these domains often promote suspicious or malicious activity

DETAILS:

- Access to tickets for the upcoming FIFA World Cup in 2026 is being conducted in phases.
- These phases start over a year before the event.
- So attackers are starting to ramp up early with fraudulent websites.
- We observed a peak in the number of FIFA World Cup-related domain registrations in June 2025.
  -- This is a year before the tournament starts in June 2026. 
- Not all registered domains are malicious, but we find suspicious behavior from them, including:
  -- monetized parking sites
  -- domain squatting,
  -- domain reselling
  -- betting
  -- gambling
  -- pirated streams
  -- distributing gambling apps and other potentially unwanted applications
- These domains resolve to cloud-hosted IP addresses, many having malicious verdicts on VirusTotal: 
  -- 13.248.169[.]48
  -- 76.223.54[.]146
  -- 104.21.96[.]1
  -- 104.21.16[.]1
  -- 104.21.112[.]1
- Squatting domains using the naming pattern worldcup20xxtickets were registered in 2007.
- These domains are generally:
  -- Acquired and transferred to new owners, or
  -- Activated just before World Cup events to capitalize on the surrounding excitement
- These unofficial ticket websites mimic secondary reselling markets:
  -- They do not validate user information beyond credit card details. 
  -- They claim tickets will be delivered through the official 2026 World Cup ticketing app.
  -- They directly collect payment information and may charge victims on their sites.
